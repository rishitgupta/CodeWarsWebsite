Testing Procedures

  • Our testing teams only conduct RT-PCR and Rapid Antibody Tests as these are verified by WHO.

  • The Testing Facility will contact you for your symptoms and other details and will make sure that a testing team reaches you within the next 24 hours.

  • Please adhere to the instructions given by the Testing Team upon their visit.

  • After the test you should receive the report within the next 12 hours.

  • If found positive, you shall be contacted for further procedures.
